PoorCoin (POOR)

The People’s Meme Token — BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)

Overview

PoorCoin (POOR) is a community-driven meme token on the BNB Smart Chain. The project focuses on transparency, fair distribution, and a simple narrative: a meme token for ordinary people building together. Liquidity is added on PancakeSwap and the contract is verified on BscScan.
