PoorCoin — Whitepaper

The community-driven meme token on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20).

Overview

PoorCoin (POOR) is a simple, community-first meme token on the BNB Smart Chain. Our goal is to keep things fun, transparent and fair while the community experiments and grows together.

  • Taxes & ownership: none taken by code; contract is a standard BEP-20.

Vision

Keep it lightweight and fair: a meme coin that’s actually owned and pushed by its community — open links, verifiable contract, simple rules. We ship small, public improvements and let holders steer the direction.

Roadmap (high-level)

  1. Phase 1: Launch site, socials, initial LP, basic listings (DEXTools).
  2. Phase 2: Community growth (Telegram/X), small bounties, improve docs (this page).
  3. Phase 3: Apply to major trackers (CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap) and light integrations.
  4. Phase 4: Utilities decided by the community; seasonal campaigns.
