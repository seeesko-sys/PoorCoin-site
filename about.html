About PoorCoin (POOR)

PoorCoin (POOR) is a meme token on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20) with a fixed total supply of 1,000,000,000 POOR. The project focuses on simple tokenomics, transparent distribution and accessibility via PancakeSwap.

Official contract: 0x6208384e2dda2ab804a5b75aebc1609ba9ce7336 (verified on BscScan).
