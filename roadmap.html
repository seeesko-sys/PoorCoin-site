Roadmap

Built by and for the community. Milestones are indicative and may shift as the community votes.

  1. Phase 0 — Launch

    • Token deployed on BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20)
    • Initial supply: 1,000,000,000 POOR
    • Website + GitHub Pages live (poorcoin.net)
    • Socials: Telegram, X (Twitter)

  2. Phase 1 — Liquidity & Basics

    • Liquidity on PancakeSwap (~12,000,000 POOR + 0.011 BNB (est.))
    • Basic analytics: BscScan, DEXTools
    • Community FAQ & Whitepaper (lite)

  3. Phase 2 — Community Growth

    • Community contests & memes
    • Organic KOL outreach & mini-campaigns
    • Website improvements, multi-lang pages

  4. Phase 3 — Listings & Utilities

    • Apply for trackers & listings (CoinGecko/CoinMarketCap)
    • Simple utilities (bots, mini-apps, holders perks)
    • Community treasury experiments

  5. Phase 4 — Expansion

    • Partnerships & cross-community events
    • Further liquidity & marketing as voted by the community
    • Iterate the roadmap publicly
